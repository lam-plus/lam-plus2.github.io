	<h1>Laboratory</h1>

	<h2>What is LAM+</h2>
	<p>LAM+ is a scientific infrastructure dedicated to the integrated analysis of sedimentary sequences. Its goal is to bring together high-resolution technologies and advanced computational tools to characterize sediments in a non-destructive, reproducible and integrated way.</p>
	<p>The laboratory combines multiple lines of evidence, including images, spectra, chemical elements, physical properties and laboratory data, into analytical workflows that support sedimentological, paleoenvironmental and climatic interpretations.</p>

	<h2>Mission and scope</h2>
	<p>To integrate scanning, imaging, compositional analysis and artificial intelligence technologies to produce high-resolution data from sedimentary sequences, fostering methodological innovation, human resource training and scientific collaboration.</p>

	<h2>Vision</h2>
	<p>To consolidate LAM+ as a reference infrastructure in multispectral analysis and artificial intelligence applied to sediments, contributing to advances in geosciences, paleoclimatology and the investigation of environmental change.</p>

	<h2>Analytical infrastructure</h2>
	<p>The laboratory integrates hyperspectral imaging, XRF core scanning, color analysis, sedimentary radiography, physical properties, electron microscopy and artificial intelligence tools to generate analytical products for sedimentological, paleoclimatic, geochemical and environmental interpretation.</p>

	<h2>Governance, people and collaborations</h2>
	<p>LAM+ brings together researchers, technical staff, students and collaborators in a multi-user and interdisciplinary structure, organized through committees and roles focused on operation, methodological development and scientific application.</p>
